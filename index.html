Privacy Policy

App: Beyblade X Companion
Last updated: May 29, 2026

Information We Collect

This app uses anonymous authentication via Firebase. No name, email, or personal information is required or collected. A random anonymous ID is generated on first launch to sync your data across devices.

Data You Create

Your collection, builds, battle records, and tournament data are stored in Firebase Firestore under your anonymous account. This data is used solely to provide app functionality and is never sold or shared with third parties.

Advertising

This app displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads. You will be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown, in accordance with applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA). You may choose non-personalized ads at any time.

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention

Your app data is retained as long as your anonymous account exists. Uninstalling the app removes local data. Cloud data may persist until manually deleted within the app.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
